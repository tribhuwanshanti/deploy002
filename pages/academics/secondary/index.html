About Education Nepali

Shree Tribhuwan Shanti Secondary School, located in the tranquil Pokhara-30, Khudi, Kaski, offers a robust secondary level education program aimed at nurturing well-rounded individuals equipped for future challenges and opportunities. At Shree Tribhuwan Shanti Secondary School, secondary level education is designed to not only impart academic knowledge but also to foster essential skills among students. Shree Tribhuwan Shanti Secondary School provides a supportive learning environment with dedicated faculty who employ modern teaching methodologies and resources. The school's infrastructure includes well-equipped classrooms, science and computer laboratories, a library with a diverse collection of academic resources, and spaces for sports and extracurricular activities. Beyond academics, the school emphasizes holistic development through sports, arts, and community service initiatives. These activities help students build teamwork, leadership skills, and a sense of social responsibility, preparing them to contribute positively to society. Overall, secondary level education at Shree Tribhuwan Shanti Secondary School in Pokhara-30, Khudi, Kaski, aims to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for personal fulfillment and success in a rapidly evolving world.
