Chairperson (M.ED)

It gives me great pleasure to extend my heartfelt congratulations and a warm welcome to all students aspiring to pursue at our esteemed institution. These developments are part of our ongoing commitment to providing a conducive learning environment for our students. we are actively working towards completing all remaining infrastructure projects outlined by the Model School Vision of the Nepal Government. Somat Kumari Lamichhane

Headteacher (M.ED)

Welcome to all prospective students and their guardians, I would like to extend my heartfelt appreciation to the SEE graduates for considering Shree Tribhuwan Shanti Secondary School (STSSS) as the paramount destination for pursuing Class Nursery to XII. At STSSS, we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and innovation in education. Our school is renowned for its advanced ICT-based teaching methodologies, designed to enhance learning experiences and outcomes. We boast a team of experienced and dedicated teaching faculties who are committed to nurturing the potential of every student. Our well-managed laboratories and libraries provide students with the resources they need to excel academically. In addition to our academic offerings, we provide a spacious playground and opportunities for competitive sports, fostering holistic development among our students. Our management team ensures a supportive and conducive learning environment that encourages both personal growth and academic success. I invite all students and their guardians to visit our campus and experience firsthand the distinct learning environment we offer at STSSS. We are confident that you will find our facilities and educational ethos aligned with your aspirations for a well-rounded and fulfilling educational journey.

Ramchandra Bhandari

Asst.teacher (ph.D Candidate)

Shree Tribhuwan Shanti Secondary School has been a cornerstone of education in Kaski, Gandaki, Nepal, for over six decades, providing comprehensive school-level education. In 2063 B.S., the school management introduced higher education programs, offering students new pathways in their academic pursuits. Recognizing the demand for English medium education in science and management, the school expanded its offerings to include Class 11 English medium classes starting in 2076 B.S. This initiative has been well-received, providing students with enhanced opportunities for learning. To support these advancements, Shree Tribhuwan Shanti Secondary School has invested in state-of-the-art infrastructure, ensuring that students have access to high-tech facilities conducive to their educational growth. The school's commitment extends beyond academics to fostering self-confidence among students, empowering them to achieve their educational goals. This holistic approach aims to prepare students not only for academic success but also for personal and professional fulfillment in their future endeavors.
