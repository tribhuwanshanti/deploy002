About The School

Shree Tribhuwan Shanti Secondary School in Kaski emphasizes humanity, cultural, moral, and spiritual values alongside academic knowledge. Our mission is to develop well-rounded, thoughtful, and confident students capable of navigating the ever-changing global landscape. We promote a culture of mutual respect and acceptance, ensuring students graduate with essential skills for their future pursuits.

Activity-based value education
Competitive learning environment
Individualized attention in studies and sports
Experienced professional team
Mind mapping and cultural exchange
Ongoing teacher induction

Our Curriculum

Pre-Primary

(Nursery To UKG)

Shree Tribhuwan Shanti pre-primary classes (Nursery to UKG) offer a secure, supportive environment with engaging activities using toys and play materials. Dedicated teachers foster a love for learning through creative play and exploration, ensuring each child feels involved and prepared.

Basic

(One To Eight)

Shree Tribhuwan Shanti basic classes offer a dynamic learning environment. Our teachers engage students with interactive activities and teaching methods, laying a foundation. Through a blend of structured learning and exploration, we cultivate a passion for knowledge and ensure comprehensive support for each student's progress."

Secondary

(Nine to XII)

Shree Tribhuwan Shanti classes for grades 9 to 12 are equipped with modern tools like smart boards and projectors. Lessons are interactive and practical, facilitated by experienced teachers who use advanced technology to simplify complex concepts. This prepares students for academic success and future endeavors.
